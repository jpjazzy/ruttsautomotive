Our location

Rutt's Automotive is located in the Marysville area, off 80th St. We are an automotive repair shop and provide top quality professional repair for all makes and models, both foreign and domestic.

Let our trained and experienced technicians handle all your auto repair and servicing needs. Give us a call today!
