About us

Rutt's Automotive is an independent automotive service repair facility. We do cover a broad range of automotive services which includes, but is not limited to, engine repair and rebuilds, clutch repairs, electrical diagnostics, transmission overhauls, and routine maintenance services. We do it all, 30k, 60k, 90k services, diagnosis, engines, transmissions, differentials, diesels, forigen and domestic.

Our technicians have extensive training and expertise with the latest equipment, so your car gets the best service possible at a price that will fit within your budget. Our technicians are constantly receiving training newer technologies as well, so we are not limited to a certain range of automotive repair.
